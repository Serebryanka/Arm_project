Armica is delighted to offer UNU power drink.

UNU Power is a functional beverage that energizes the body and stimulates the mind. It is specially developed for the periods of increased mental and physical exertion.

UNU Power has become a product of choice for men and women who desire to be more productive and alert in their day-to-day activities. The drink can be consumed in virtually any situation: during sports, at work, whilst driving and during leisure.

UNU Power is an enjoyable beverage which proved to deliver maximum energy and stamina. Due to unique chemical composition it provides full flavour without unpleasant aftertaste. It is packed with vitamins and minerals, which makes it a healthy alternative to an average energy drink.

UNU Power is suitable for vegetarians. It does not contain any animal products or substances derived from animals.

UNU Power increases physical and mental performance, improves vigilance when close and continuous attention is required, stimulates metabolism to prolong endurance of toughest challenges, increases concentration and reaction speed.​​
